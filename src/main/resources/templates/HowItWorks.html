Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my business logo?
Navigate to 'My Profile' in the sidebar. Click on the business logo area to upload your official PNG or JPG file. This logo will automatically appear on all Product and Service invoices.
Is the inventory deduction automatic?
Yes! As soon as you click 'Finalize & Paid' or 'Finalize & Due' for a Product-Based bill, the system instantly subtracts those quantities from your specific product stock levels.
Can I edit a bill after it's been finalized?
To maintain audit integrity, 'Finalized' bills cannot be edited. However, 'Draft' bills can be modified as many times as needed before finalization.
What is the difference between Product-Based and Service-Based bills?
Product-Based bills track physical quantities and HSN codes, automatically deducting from inventory. Service-Based bills are for labor or consulting and use SAC codes without stock deduction.
How do I generate an E-Way Bill?
Select the 'E-Way Bill' option from the sidebar, enter the transport details (Vehicle Number, Transporter ID), and the system will generate a compliant document linked to your invoice data.
Can I track internal system-wide health as an Admin?
Yes. Admins have a 'Global Dashboard' that aggregates revenue across all owners, monitors server response times, and shows recent registration activity.
Does the system support multiple GST tiers?
Absolutely. You can set individual GST percentages (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) for each product or service during the inventory setup phase.
How can I handle 'Partially Paid' bills?
When finalizing a bill, entering an amount less than the total will mark the invoice as 'DUE'. You can later update the payment from the Dashboard's 'Outstanding Payments' widget.
Are my reports exportable to Excel or PDF?
Yes, every invoice and detailed financial report can be downloaded as a PDF. Advanced data exports for tax filing (CSV) are available in the Reports section.
What happens if I reject a registration request?
Rejected requests are deleted from the pending queue. The requester will not be able to log in, but they can submit a new request if needed.
Is there an alert for 'Inventory Low'?
Yes, the system highlights products in red on your Manage Stocks page when they fall below the safety threshold.
Can I search for a specific invoice number?
Yes, both the Dashboard and the Reports page feature a real-time search bar where you can filter by Invoice #, Client Name, or Amount.
What is the 'Broadcast System'?
Administrators can send real-time alerts or announcements that appear immediately on every live Business Owner's dashboard via a scrolling header marquee.
How do I delete an item I no longer sell?
Go to 'Manage Stocks', find the item, and click the delete icon. Note that this won't remove the item from past invoices to preserve historical data.
Is the system mobile responsive?
Yes, the software is built with a mobile-first approach. You can manage inventory and view reports comfortably on any smartphone or tablet.

Pro Tips

  • Use the "Draft" feature to save quotations and finalize them when the sale is confirmed.
